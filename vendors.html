Vendor & Booth Opportunities

Sell your hand-carved products, tools, crafts, or related items directly to thousands of attendees at CARVEFEST 2026.

Standard Booth – $300

Great for small artisans and creators.

  • 10x10 ft space
  • 1 table & 2 chairs
  • Listing on event website
  • Electricity access (extra fee)
Premium Booth – $550

Prime location for higher visibility.

  • 10x20 ft corner space
  • 2 tables & 4 chairs
  • Priority placement near main stage
  • Featured on social media
  • Included electricity
Food/Food-Related – $450

For vendors offering Bajan treats or carving-themed refreshments.

  • Food-safe setup space
  • Access to water/power
  • Health & safety compliance required

Booth Rules & Guidelines

  • All vendors must be approved; no unauthorized sales allowed.
  • Setup on Feb 26 evening; event hours Fri–Sun 9 AM – 8 PM.
  • Products must align with carving, craft, culture, or agriculture theme.
  • No open flames unless pre-approved; insurance certificate required for food vendors.
  • Keep area clean; disposal fees apply for excess waste.
  • Payment due upon approval; non-refundable after Jan 31, 2026.
