Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about CARVEFEST 2026.

Where is CARVEFEST 2026 held?

At "The Grotto", Beckles Road, St. Michael, Bridgetown – inside the AGROFEST grounds.

What are the event hours?

Friday Feb 27 – Sunday Mar 1, 2026: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily.

Is there parking available?

Yes, free parking is available on-site and nearby. Arrive early on peak days. Follow signage for designated lots.

What happens if it rains?

Most activities (demonstrations, market) are under covered areas or tents. Workshops proceed indoors if needed. No full cancellation unless extreme weather.

What security measures are in place?

On-site security, bag checks at entry, first aid station, and lost & found. Follow staff instructions at all times.

What payment options are accepted?

Cash (BBD/USD), credit/debit cards, mobile payments (where available), and online booking for tickets/workshops.

Are tickets refundable?

Tickets are non-refundable but transferable. Contact us for special circumstances.
