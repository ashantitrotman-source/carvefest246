CARVEFEST – Embracing Culture – Sustainable Industry

A national woodcarving experience that brings Barbadian heritage to life - creating real opportunities for skills development, business growth, and sustainable livelihoods.

Hosted within AGROFEST, Barbados, CARVEFEST brings together master carvers, artisans, young apprentices, buyers, producers, and partner brands in one vibrant space. It’s where tradition meets opportunity - through live carving demonstrations using both hand tools and power tools, youth training experiences, meaningful conversations, and partnerships that help turn craft into lasting careers. Whether you’re curious about carving as a hobby, exploring creative business opportunities, or simply love beautifully made things, there’s something at CARVEFEST for you.

What you will experience:

  • Watch master carvers shape mahogany in real time
  • See high-energy chainsaw carving and large-scale sculptures
  • Discover one-of-a-kind Caribbean pieces you can buy and take home
  • Meet artisans and hear the stories behind their work
  • Perfect for visitors looking to take home a piece of Barbados’s mahogany.
